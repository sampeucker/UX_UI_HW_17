Trail talk was born to solve the problems that many hikers face. Lack of real-time information. The app aims to provide hikers with the most up to date trail information such as trail condition, Current weather, How busy it is and real time water supply information. This was all packaged in a way to embody the conversation that you would have with a fellow hiker on your way past them. Hence the name “Trail Talk”.

Initially the problem was raised.

“People who enjoy getting out in nature need a better way to understand and plan nature walks and hikes nearby to their location”

To understand this problem users were asked questions with these 3 objectives in mind.

Understand the motivations as to why people hike in the first place and how they like their hikes to be

Find out what information people find important when embarking on a hike

Discover what equipment and tools people find useful when hiking (gadgets, clothes, tools)

The results from 6 user interviews were recorded and reduced down to a few key points and a User persona was created.

Click to enlarge

From here the problem was clear.

People spend a long time researching which natural trail to walk in their spare time to optimise their experience/connection with nature and ensure that they are well-prepared for trail conditions. Because different states, areas and parks have inconsistent rules and regulations, it is difficult for hikers to know where to go for the most up-to-date information on the trail conditions, necessary equipment and water availability. This is a problem because, when hikers can not find the relevant information it undermines their main motivation for taking a hike, turning a pleasent experience into a stressful (and sometimes dangerous) situation.

Some key features were ideated upon to solve the problems of the user. A final app proposition was defined including these features to present to the stakeholders.

Our team is developing a database-and-community-driven social information app to assist hikers of all varieties with researching, planning and preparing for upcoming hikes of their interest, so that they can easily achieve their hiking goals safely and responsibly.

Our app differentiates itself from competitors by only offering necessary and precise information that is regulated by only the most-recent users, rather than simply offering testimonials.

We're believable because our information comes directly from humans who have just experienced the trail conditions and are motivated to share their experience, so that others may benefit from the information provided in the same way that they did themselves.
